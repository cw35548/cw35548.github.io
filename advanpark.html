AdvanPark

Overview

AdvanPark is my favorite project idea of all five. It comes from my personal experience and has two main functions. AdvanPark combines different transits and provides more reasonable suggestions for different user needs. In addiction, this is an O2O platform especially for matching long-term parking spaces.

Combination of Options

We combine transportation platforms (car/train/walking). Based on your preference (driving, public transportation, uber or lyft), we suggest you the best solution.

Real-time Smart Parking

We tell you what transportation you could take to avoid the traffic and suggest you the possible parking spaces in the most convenient points on your way to the destination.

Encourage Posting

For parking space owners, we match parking spaces and really solve the pain points for both sides.

Create Win-win

We protect the environment, reduce energy waste and traffic while searching for parking. We save time and money for users. We bring extra income for providers.
