StyleMax

Overview

While building the official website and being the social media marketing instructor for nearly 100 designers in Rich Hair Salon Group, I fully understand the pain points from both designer side and management side. The problem was originally raised by the GM and CEO, and I make it into StyleMax and implement part of it with D3.

How this idea comes out

I dealt with not only the CEO and general manager, but all different kinds of haor stylists and designers. I came to have a deep understanding of the ecosystem of this industry and the pain points of most of designers.

Something you need to know

This is a very unique industry. Customers love to share their personal life with designers during the service. Sometimes customers not coming here for the unreplacable service or skills, but for chatting. So, designers not only have skills, but are forced to do whatever they could to get closer to customers.

What we offer

This is an app for client oriented designers in beauty industry. With StyleMax, you will easily upgrade your service, improve customer relationship, deeply understand their preference and, in the end, optimize all collected data to help more effectively market your brand image.

What bothers them most

Most designers pay a huge percentage of their salary to join chain enterprises for its fame and benefits, so that they can only focus on professional skills and clients. But the truth is over 70% of designers deeply feel they paid so much but gained little and they have no other choice.
