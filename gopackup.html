Gopackup

Overview

This was my first real-world development experience in the US as an UX designer. I like this idea and appreciate the chance to focus on the UX research, WBS, competitor analysis, user testing, wireframe and prototype building. In the end, I came out prototypes for the sections: “home page, make a tour process, guide’s shop & management page, and user searching process.”

About

Gopackup is a trusted O2O platform established in 2012. This marketplace is for individuals to create unique tours based on their own perceptions of a local city and offer their services to global travelers.

Mission

Gopackup aims to connect travelers with locals based on shared interests to facilitate in-depth exploration of a city. It's mission is to create convenient and memorable experience for both travelers and local guides.

Challenge

For me, the most challenging part is to decide the top main category for "Make a Tour" section and the scheduling setting for different tours. I tried four different user flows to get the better result.

Collaboration

This was a great opportunity to closely cooperate and discuss with marketing and development team at a fast-paced early stage startup. In the past, I used different approach to associate with (outsource to) other studios. It's a valuable experience.
