Helper

Overview

This project was inspired by my personal experience while taking care of elders and my previous hospital project understanding the complexityof medical system in Taiwan. Due to the complexity of the government regulations and opaqueness of caretaker agencies, many patients and family members suffered. Helper is the ideal interactive tool to break the current status and create the win-win situation.

Survey Data

Based on 52 groups of family survey at Taiwan University Hospital and Chung Shan Hospital.
